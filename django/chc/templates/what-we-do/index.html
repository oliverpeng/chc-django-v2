The best way to start rebuilding a church
is to rebuild the pastor.

Taking the gospel into grief, CHP is a counseling initiative focused on ministering to and equipping pastors who are both leaders and victims during crisis.

The church is not a building.

A building allows the people of God to gather without distraction, even during inclement weather. But our reconstruction projects are about so much more than the building.

Getting the Church back on its feet.

Sometimes all it takes for a ministry to get going again is a small apartment for a pastor or a rental space to meet in or a bicycle to visit community members.

CHC engages in additional miscellaneous projects that
will help the church.

Every disaster is unique - As we have opportunity, CHC takes on miscellaneous projects and awards grants that will help the church. To see a list of completed projects click on a flag at the bottom of this page.
