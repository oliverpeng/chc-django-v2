The best way to start rebuilding a church
is to rebuild the pastor.

CHP is a Gospel-centered grief counseling initiative focused on treating and training pastors who are leaders and victims after a crisis.

The church is more than just a building.

CHC helps churches rebuild their structures so the people of God can worship together under a roof and out of the weather, but our reconstruction projects are about so much more than just the building.

Getting the Church back on its feet.

Sometimes all it takes for a ministry to get going again is a small apartment for a pastor or a rental space to meet in or a bicycle to visit community members.

CHC engages in additional miscellaneous projects that
will help the church.
