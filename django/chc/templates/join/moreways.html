Send Prayer Book

Prayer is the most effective way to help churches after a disaster. Create a custom prayer book with your church or group that will be delivered to a pastor in a crisis area.

For more information, contact us at information@churcheshelpingchurches.com.

Experience CHC Live

Interested in sharing Churches Helping Churches with your congregation or group? Allow us to host an evening of fellowship, encouragement and praise that will challenge your community of believers.

For details, please contact David Shin at dshin@churcheshelpingchurches.com.

Partner with a Church

For churches that want to engage deeper, Church-to-Church partnership offers a long-term relationship with a church in crisis that is not just about building projects but about building relationships.

Find more information download the Executive Summary on Church-to-Church partnerships or contact us at information@churcheshelpingchurches.com.
